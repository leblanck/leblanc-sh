F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What shuold I wear?

    Since this is not a wedding ceremony, we are encouraging you to dress in an elevated fall vibe. We are hoping to create a cozy and welcoming atmosphere and want you to be comfortably cute.

  • Can I bring my children?

    While we love your children (and your fur babies), our celebration is for adults only.

  • Where can I park?

    Apres is located in the East Bayside neighborhood of Portland and there is street parking. We encourage carpooling or ubering to the venue if you are able.

  • Will there be food?

    Yes! Food will be served, but we will not have a formal seated meal.

  • Are gifts accepted?

    Your presence at our celebration is the greatest gift of all, but if we are honored with a gift from you, a monetary gift will be very much appreciated. In addition to being new homeowners, we are saving for the honeymoon of our dreams for our five year anniversary.
